# dekitayo - Privacy Policy

**v1.0.0 / Effective Date: 2026-05-12  Last Updated: 2026-05-12**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We and our analytics providers (TelemetryDeck and Firebase Analytics) may collect limited usage, performance, and crash information, including:

- App events and feature usage
- Device type, operating system version, and approximate region
- App version and build
- Pseudonymous app or device identifiers
- Crash diagnostics and stability data

**We do not use this information to identify you or your child personally.**

**Information We Do NOT Collect (stored locally only)**:

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to servers operated by us:
- Your child's name and date of birth
- Milestone records, photos, and comments

> **Note on backups**: Depending on your device settings, operating system features, and Apple services you use, your local data may be included in iCloud backups, device backups, or device transfers. We do not control those services.

---

## 2. Purpose of Use

- Service delivery and feature improvement (bug analysis, quality improvement, etc.)
- Notifications and support responses
- Compliance with laws and addressing violations of terms of service

---

## 3. How We Collect Information

We collect information through in-app operations, device functions, and analytics/crash reporting SDKs (TelemetryDeck) that we use.

---

## 4. Retention Period

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, and delete or anonymize it when no longer needed.

---

## 5. Third-Party Sharing and Outsourcing

We outsource analytics to a third party (TelemetryDeck). We require appropriate contractual and security measures from our service providers. Any third-party sharing will be conducted in accordance with applicable laws.

**Service Provider**:
- TelemetryDeck: Collection and analysis of anonymized usage statistics
  - Privacy Policy: https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/

## 6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by parents and guardians, not by children directly. Children aged 0-3 are the subject of milestone records, not users of the app. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you are a minor, please use this service with the consent of your parent or guardian.
